# 🏕️ Park Pass Explorer

An interactive map of all NPS sites and federal recreation areas in the continental United States — including which sites are covered by the **America the Beautiful Annual Pass**, which have free entry, and which belong to the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service, U.S. Forest Service, and Bureau of Land Management.

**[View Live Map →](https://WilliamCWu.github.io/national-park-map)**

---

## Features

- 🗺️ **Interactive map** powered by MapLibre GL with clustering and popups
- 🔍 **Search** by park name, state, or site type
- 🏷️ **Filter by agency** — NPS (Pass), NPS (Free), USFWS, USFS, BLM
- 🌎 **Filter by region** — Northeast, Southeast, Midwest, West, Southwest
- 💰 **Fee details** — vehicle and per-person entrance fees shown for each site
- 🌙 **Dark mode** — auto-detects system preference with manual toggle
- 📱 **Mobile responsive** — collapsible sidebar for small screens

## Data Sources

Site data sourced from official agency websites as of March 2026:

- [NPS.gov](https://www.nps.gov) — National Park Service
- [USFWS.gov](https://www.fws.gov) — U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service
- [USFS.gov](https://www.fs.usda.gov) — U.S. Forest Service
- [BLM.gov](https://www.blm.gov) — Bureau of Land Management

## About the America the Beautiful Pass

The **America the Beautiful – National Parks and Federal Recreational Lands Annual Pass** costs $80/year and covers entrance fees at thousands of federal recreation sites. This map helps you visualize where your pass works and identify free-entry NPS sites that don't require it.

More info: [store.usgs.gov/pass](https://store.usgs.gov/pass)
